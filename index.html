Project Overview

Exploring the triumphs of Paris 2024 Olympics

This is an exploratory data analysis on the results from the Paris 2024 Olympics. The exploration aims to view trends of medals across various sports activities in different countries. By analyzing this data, we seek to observe the number of participants per event and country and the total medals won in each.

Data Source

The database used for this analysis is 'Olympics_2024.csv', containing detailed information about medals won, medalists, countries, and more.

Tools

  • Excel - Data cleaning
  • Power BI - Visualization and reports

Results/Findings

  1. USA had the highest medals: 111
  2. USA had the highest medal points: 216, making it the winner of the 2024 Paris Olympics.
  3. China had the second-highest medal points: 176.
  4. Athletics and Swimming had the highest number of medals: 112 and 105, respectively.
  5. The USA had the highest number of athletes for the 2024 Olympics, with over 600 athletes. They participated in 47 disciplines out of 49, which could account for the high number of medals won by the USA.
